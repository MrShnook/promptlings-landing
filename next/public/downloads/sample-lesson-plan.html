5 min
Warm-Up: "What Do You Think AI Is?"
Open discussion â€” students share what they know about AI. Most will mention Siri, Alexa, or robots. We meet them where they are.

Core Lesson: AI is a Tool, Not Magic
We explain AI with kid-friendly analogies ("a friend who read every book in every library"), demo a live AI conversation, and introduce the key idea: AI is helpful but not perfect.

Each student asks AI 5 questions (mix of fun and learning), then rates each answer. They discover firsthand that AI can be creative, helpful, and sometimes wrong.

Interactive thumbs-up/down game teaching what's okay to share with AI (favorite color = yes, home address = no). One rule they'll never forget.
5 min

Quick review, then homework: interview a family member about AI. Brings the conversation home and gets parents involved from day one.
